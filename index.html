case studies

Government Contract Breach

The client lost a mining concession in a South American country to a less qualified candidate. Investigated the cluster of companies connected to the candidate and found government commission members benefiting from one of the companies.

These members were linked to the political fringe of the former President of the country. Due to the competitor's interests in the USA and Europe and the high corruption level in the country, the investigation focused on government officials connected to the client's counterpart.

A comprehensive report on the corrupt activities of the former President reached a US Congressional Committee. The report was used as a primary source to sanction the former President under the Magnitsky Act, leading to the client being able to resume their concession while the counterpart couldn't engage in business with the former President.
